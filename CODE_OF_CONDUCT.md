# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, sexual identity and orientation, or other protected characteristic.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable technical feedback, and keep
discussion focused on improving the project. Harassment, discrimination,
personal attacks, doxxing, and sustained disruption are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or edit contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation for behavior they consider inappropriate.
Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through GitHub. Maintainers
will respect the reporter's privacy as far as possible.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
